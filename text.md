# Media Query Breakpoints

## Small Devices (Phones)
- **Max Width:** 600px

## Medium Devices (Tablets)
- **Min Width:** 600px
- **Max Width:** 768px

## Large Devices (Desktops)
- **Min Width:** 768px
- **Max Width:** 1024px

## Extra Large Devices (Large Desktops)
- **Min Width:** 1024px
